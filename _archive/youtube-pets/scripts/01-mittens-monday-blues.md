# Episode 01 — Mittens: Monday Morning Diary

## Meta
- **Character**: Mittens (grumpy British cat)
- **Title**: "Day 47 in the Human Prison"
- **Duration**: 50s
- **Scenario**: Sardonic comedy
- **Hook style**: Dramatic monologue + visual judging stare

## English Script

### HOOK (0-2s)
**Visual**: Extreme close-up of Mittens' copper eyes slowly opening, glaring directly at camera. Soft morning window light. Single judgmental blink.
**Audio**: Distant alarm clock buzzing (foley)
**Voice (Charlotte BR female, sardonic)**:
> "Day forty-seven. The slave's alarm has gone off again."

### SETUP (2-12s)
**Visual**: Pull back to show Mittens lying perfectly still on a pillow next to a sleeping human. Morning bedroom, slightly messy.
**Voice**:
> "I've calculated the optimal moment to strike. He always presses the snooze button. Pathetic. So predictable."

### ESCALATION (12-30s)
**Visual**: Beat 1 — Mittens slowly walks across human's face. Beat 2 — Sits directly on human's chest. Beat 3 — Stares unblinking at human's closed eyes.
**Voice**:
> "Phase one: occupy his airway. Phase two: claim the warm spot. Phase three: maintain eye contact until he questions his life choices."

### TWIST (30-42s)
**Visual**: Human suddenly opens eyes, smiles, scratches Mittens behind ears. Mittens' grumpy expression slowly... softens. Almost imperceptibly.
**Voice**:
> "Wait. He's... rubbing the spot. Behind the ears. The forbidden spot."
> *[pause 0.5s]*
> "Damn it."

### PAYOFF (42-50s)
**Visual**: Mittens reluctantly purring, eyes half-closed in pleasure but trying to look annoyed.
**Voice**:
> "I yield. Today only. Tomorrow we resume hostilities."

### CTA (50-55s)
**Visual**: Mittens glares at camera, narrows eyes.
**Voice**:
> "Subscribe. Or I'll know."
