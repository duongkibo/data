# Episode 02 — Biscuit: First Snow

## Meta
- **Character**: Biscuit (optimistic golden retriever)
- **Title**: "I Have Discovered God's Dandruff"
- **Duration**: 45s
- **Scenario**: Heartwarming comedy
- **Hook style**: Visual wonder + dramatic reveal

## English Script

### HOOK (0-2s)
**Visual**: Extreme close-up of Biscuit's wide brown eyes, mouth open in shock, single snowflake landing on his nose. Background pure white blur.
**Audio**: Quiet wind, soft snowfall
**Voice (Adam, warm enthusiastic male)**:
> "Wait. WAIT. What is this... white sky food?"

### SETUP (2-12s)
**Visual**: Pull back to show Biscuit sitting in snow-covered backyard. He's frozen, watching snowflakes fall around him. Tilts head left, then right.
**Voice**:
> "Day eight hundred and twelve of being a dog. I thought I had seen everything. The mailman. The vacuum. That suspicious squirrel."

### ESCALATION (12-28s)
**Visual**: Beat 1 — Biscuit cautiously sniffs ground. Beat 2 — Boops a snowflake with nose. Beat 3 — Eyes widen in pure amazement. Beat 4 — Big jump into snow pile, full body wiggle.
**Voice**:
> "But this... THIS is new. It's cold. It's wet. It TASTES LIKE NOTHING. Which means..."
> *[gasp]*
> "...it must be EVERYTHING."

### TWIST (28-40s)
**Visual**: Biscuit running in circles through snow, throwing it up with his nose, completely euphoric. Slow motion paw splashes.
**Voice**:
> "I have been chosen. The sky has rained joy upon me specifically. I am the special boy. The CHOSEN GOOD BOY."

### PAYOFF (40-48s)
**Visual**: Biscuit lies down in snow, panting happily, looking up at falling snowflakes with reverence.
**Voice**:
> "Today is the best day. Tomorrow will also be the best day. Every day is the best day, actually."

### CTA (48-52s)
**Visual**: Biscuit tilts head at camera, snowflake on nose, big goofy smile.
**Voice**:
> "Subscribe? It would make me VERY excited."
