# Episode 04 — Pixel: The Amazon Box Discovery

## Meta
- **Character**: Pixel (chaotic kitten)
- **Title**: "I Have Found My Kingdom"
- **Duration**: 40s
- **Scenario**: Chaotic comedy
- **Hook style**: Visual chaos + breathless voice

## English Script

### HOOK (0-2s)
**Visual**: Extreme close-up Pixel's wide yellow eyes staring into a brown cardboard Amazon box, completely dilated pupils.
**Audio**: Cat squeak / chirp sound
**Voice (Emily, hyper female)**:
> "Wait WAIT WAIT what is THIS—"

### SETUP (2-10s)
**Visual**: Pixel circling the cardboard box, sniffing every corner at high speed. Living room background with knocked-over plant in frame edge.
**Voice**:
> "Brown thing. SMELLS like outside. Has FLAPS. Has CORNERS. The corners go forever. Where do the corners go?!"

### ESCALATION (10-28s)
**Visual**: Beat 1 — Pixel jumps INTO box, disappears. Beat 2 — Head pops up. Beat 3 — Disappears again. Beat 4 — Box wiggles violently from inside. Beat 5 — Pixel emerges with cardboard scrap on head.
**Voice**:
> "I'm in the box. I'm in the BOX. I AM THE BOX. The box is me. We are one entity now. Together we shall—WAIT WHAT WAS THAT?"
> *[gasp]*
> "Was that a SOUND? Did the BOX speak?"

### TWIST (28-36s)
**Visual**: Pixel solemnly sits on top of box, paw raised. Looks majestic for half a second, then immediately falls off the side.
**Voice**:
> "I claim this box in the name of all small creatures. This is now my—"
> *[falls]*
> "—I MEANT TO DO THAT."

### PAYOFF (36-40s)
**Visual**: Pixel lying upside down next to box, all four paws in the air, completely satisfied.
**Voice**:
> "Best day of my life. So far."

### CTA (40-44s)
**Visual**: Pixel pops up suddenly from behind box.
**Voice**:
> "SUBSCRIBE. I HAVE MORE BOXES TO CONQUER."
