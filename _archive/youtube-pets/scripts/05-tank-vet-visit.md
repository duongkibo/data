# Episode 05 — Tank: The Betrayal

## Meta
- **Character**: Tank (lazy cynical pug)
- **Title**: "I Was Promised a Walk"
- **Duration**: 50s
- **Scenario**: Deadpan comedy
- **Hook style**: Bored dramatic statement

## English Script

### HOOK (0-2s)
**Visual**: Extreme close-up Tank's big tired pug eyes, looking directly at camera. Single dramatic blink. Background blurred sterile vet office.
**Audio**: Distant dog whining
**Voice (Antoni, deep bored male)**:
> "I have been deceived."

### SETUP (2-12s)
**Visual**: Pull back to show Tank sitting on cold metal vet exam table, unimpressed expression. Vet office background.
**Voice**:
> "This morning. The human said the magic word. Walk. WALK. We were going on a walk."
> *[pause 0.5s]*
> "This is not a walk."

### ESCALATION (12-32s)
**Visual**: Beat 1 — Tank looks left at scary medical equipment. Beat 2 — Looks right at jar of cotton swabs. Beat 3 — Looks down at floor (too tall to jump). Beat 4 — Looks back at camera with disappointment.
**Voice**:
> "I see needles. I smell other dogs' fear. There is a small woman with cold hands approaching. She has THE THERMOMETER."
> *[pause 0.5s]*
> "I require minimal effort. AND DIGNITY. Both have been denied."

### TWIST (32-44s)
**Visual**: Tank slowly looks up at camera with the most disappointed pug face possible. Single tear (or just a sad blink).
**Voice**:
> "The human made eye contact with me. He KNOWS what he did. He is currently pretending to read a magazine."
> *[pause 0.5s]*
> "There will be consequences. The consequences are: I will not look at him for forty seconds when we get home."

### PAYOFF (44-48s)
**Visual**: Tank flops dramatically onto his side on the exam table.
**Voice**:
> "This is the worst day of my entire life. Probably."

### CTA (48-52s)
**Visual**: Tank slowly lifts head, looks at camera with one tired eye.
**Voice**:
> "Subscribe. So I am not alone in my suffering."
