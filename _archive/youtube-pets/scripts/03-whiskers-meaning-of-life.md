# Episode 03 — Whiskers: The Meaning of Life

## Meta
- **Character**: Whiskers (philosophical Maine Coon)
- **Title**: "I Have Solved the Universe"
- **Duration**: 55s
- **Scenario**: Philosophical comedy
- **Hook style**: Profound statement + slow zoom

## English Script

### HOOK (0-2s)
**Visual**: Extreme close-up Whiskers' green eyes, slow blink. Sitting on ancient-looking bookshelf, dust particles in window light.
**Audio**: Single Tibetan bowl chime
**Voice (Brian, slow wise male)**:
> "I have, after much consideration, solved the meaning of existence."

### SETUP (2-15s)
**Visual**: Pull back slowly to show Whiskers sitting majestically on a stack of philosophy books. Morning sun streaming through dusty window. He looks contemplative.
**Voice**:
> "For seven cat years, I have observed this household. I have studied the humans. The patterns. The rituals. The strange box that shows moving pictures."

### ESCALATION (15-35s)
**Visual**: Beat 1 — Whiskers looks at TV showing news. Beat 2 — Looks at human typing on laptop. Beat 3 — Looks at empty food bowl. Beat 4 — Looks back at camera with profound expression.
**Voice**:
> "The humans worry. They struggle. They chase numbers on screens. They worry about something called 'the future.'"
> *[pause 1s]*
> "But I... I have figured it out."

### TWIST (35-48s)
**Visual**: Whiskers slow blink, then walks deliberately to his food bowl which is full. Looks at camera. Slowly begins eating with great satisfaction.
**Voice**:
> "The meaning of life... is a full bowl. A warm patch of sun. A nap with no interruptions."
> *[pause 0.5s]*
> "Everything else is humans being dramatic."

### PAYOFF (48-53s)
**Visual**: Whiskers in a sunbeam, eyes half closed, purring softly.
**Voice**:
> "You're welcome."

### CTA (53-57s)
**Visual**: Single slow blink at camera.
**Voice**:
> "Subscribe... if you remember to."
