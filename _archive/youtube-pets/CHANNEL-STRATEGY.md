## ❓ Q1: Niche cụ thể nào trong "pet content"?

| Pet fails compilation | 🔴 Extreme | High | ❌ Cần real footage |
| Anthropomorphic stories | 🟢 Low | **Very High** | ✅ AI-perfect |

### ✅ Decision: **"Inner Monologue Comedy + Anthropomorphic Stories"**
**Lý do**:
- AI có thể gen 100% (không cần pet thật)
- Competition thấp nhất so với 5 sub-niche
- Comedy + storytelling = high retention
- Endless content (fictional)
- Easy character consistency với AI

## ❓ Q3: Format chuẩn của 1 video?

### Format spec
- **Duration**: 35-55 giây (sweet spot research-backed: 50-60s = 76% watch-through)
- **Aspect ratio**: 9:16 vertical
- **Resolution**: 1080×1920 minimum, 1440×2560 ideal
- **Audio**: AI voice (ElevenLabs character) + ambient + music bed
- **Subtitle**: English burned-in (large bold font, hook color highlight)
- **Hook**: 1.5s đầu visual + verbal pattern interrupt
- **Structure**: Hook → Setup → Twist → Punchline → CTA

### Storytelling beat structure (35-55s)

```
0-2s    HOOK         "Wait for it" or shocking opener
2-8s    SETUP        Establish situation (cat at vet / dog seeing snow)
8-25s   ESCALATION   Inner monologue building tension/comedy
25-40s  TWIST        Unexpected reveal or punchline
40-50s  PAYOFF       Emotional/comedic resolution
50-55s  CTA          "Subscribe for more pet thoughts"
```

## ❓ Q5: Real pets hay 100% AI?

### Pros AI 100%
- Consistent character mọi episode
- No animal welfare concerns
- Endless content
- Match với current AI workflow của bồ
- Scale infinite

### Cons AI 100%
- Risk uncanny valley nếu prompt sai
- AI animal motion vẫn awkward 04/2026
- Viewer skeptical "đây có phải AI không"

### ✅ Decision: **100% AI** với mitigation:
1. Start image: **Flux 1.1 Pro Ultra** (best photoreal pet)
2. Video: **Kling 2.5** (motion) + **Veo 3** (talking head pet với lip sync)
3. Anti-CGI prompts (xem `REALISM-PROMPTS.md`)
4. Embrace "stylized hyperreal" thay vì 100% real (đỡ uncanny)
5. Comedy framing che lấp imperfection

## ❓ Q6: Language strategy?

### Options
- **A**: 100% English (global reach, RPM cao)
- **B**: 100% Vietnamese (local, low competition)
- **C**: English audio + Vietnamese subs (hybrid)

### ✅ Decision: **English audio + English burned subs + Vietnamese subs (CC track)**
- English audio = global reach + high RPM ($1-5 per 1k views vs $0.1-0.5 VN)
- English burned subs (always-on) = work without sound
- Vietnamese CC track = local viewers can enable
- YouTube auto-translate subs sang 100+ languages = max reach

## ❓ Q7: Cadence?

### Research data
- Top creators: 18-22 shorts/month (~1/day)
- Average: 7/month
- Growth threshold: 6 tháng consistent → +44%

### ✅ Decision: **1 short/day in tháng 1-2, 2/day từ tháng 3+**

**Daily cadence**:
- Post time: **Globally 18:00 EST** (peak US prime time, US RPM cao nhất)
- Sub-time VN: 06:00 sáng hôm sau (post-prep ngày hôm trước)
- Frequency: 1/day → 30 shorts/tháng

### Production timing
- Dùng **batch production** — 1 ngày gen 7 shorts → schedule post
- Saturday: review + plan tuần mới

## ❓ Q8: Hook strategy — làm sao stop scroll trong 1.5s?

### Hook patterns hiệu quả nhất cho pet shorts (data-driven)
1. **"Wait for it"** — Tạo curiosity gap
2. **Dramatic statement** — "Today is the day I betray my human"
3. **Question** — "Have you ever wondered what your cat really thinks of you?"
4. **Visual pattern interrupt** — Pet trong situation lạ (cat in suit, dog with phone)
5. **Sound trigger** — Squeaky toy / cat meow trong 0.5s đầu

### ✅ Default formula
```
1.5s đầu: VISUAL hook (pet trong unusual situation) + AUDIO hook (sound trigger)
0-2s: Voice deliver dramatic line establishing inner monologue
```

**Example**:
```
0-1.5s  VISUAL: Cat sitting on owner's keyboard, glaring at camera
0.3s    AUDIO: Sharp cat meow
1.5s    VOICE: "Day 47 in this place they call home. The slave is at it again..."
```

## ❓ Q9: Monetization — kiếm tiền từ đâu?

### Revenue stream stack

- **Tháng 1-3**: Build audience, $0 revenue
- **Tháng 4-6**: YPP qualified, $50-300/tháng
- **Tháng 7-12**: Affiliate + merch, $500-3000/tháng
- **Năm 2**: Sponsorships, $3000-10000/tháng

## ❓ Q10: Differentiator — tại sao subscribe channel này?

### Competitive analysis
- Existing AI pet channels: chủ yếu lip-sync cat singing (1 trick)
- Real pet channels: dependent on pet personality, slow content
- Pet meme accounts: random, no story arc

### ✅ Differentiator: **Recurring characters with deep personalities**
- Viewer subscribe để xem **Mittens episode mới** (như TV show)
- 5 characters → 5 fan tribes → high binge rate
- Series concept: "Mittens vs Tank" rivalry, "Biscuit's first time" series, etc.
- **Lore-building** giống TV cartoon

→ Đây là moat dài hạn. Single-clip channels không scale, character-driven channels scale infinite.
