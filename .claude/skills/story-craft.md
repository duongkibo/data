# Story Craft — Kỹ thuật văn chương nâng cấp kịch bản

## Khi nào dùng skill này
- Viết script mới (trước khi finalize)
- Revise script cũ (upgrade quality)
- Audit script đã viết (tìm chỗ flat)
- User yêu cầu "nâng cấp văn phong" / "viết hay hơn" / "thêm chiều sâu"

## 🎯 10 kỹ thuật văn chương bắt buộc áp dụng

### 1. ẨN DỤ (Metaphor) — Biến vật thể thành biểu tượng

**Rule**: Mỗi tập phải có ≥3 ẩn dụ chạy xuyên suốt (recurring metaphor / motif).

**Kỹ thuật**:
- **Object-as-character**: Vật trở thành nhân vật thứ 3 (chiếc nhẫn = lời nói dối có hình dạng)
- **Extended metaphor**: Kéo dài ẩn dụ qua nhiều beats (beat 1: nhẫn sáng → beat 4: nhẫn tối → beat 8: nhẫn bị bỏ lại)
- **Dead metaphor revival**: Lấy cụm từ quen → twist nghĩa ("She closed the laptop. She closed a chapter." → laptop = chapter of life)

**Ví dụ áp dụng cho Karma**:
```
❌ Flat: "Sarah found out the ring was fake."
✅ Metaphor: "The ring had always been telling her the truth. She just hadn't learned its language yet."

❌ Flat: "Daniel was lying."
✅ Metaphor: "Daniel had been building a house with two front doors. Sarah had been living in one. Rachel had been living in the other. Neither knew about the hallway between them."
```

### 2. HOÁN DỤ (Metonymy) — Gọi tên bằng chi tiết liên quan

**Rule**: Thay vì gọi thẳng cảm xúc, gọi bằng vật/hành động liên quan.

**Kỹ thuật**:
- **Synecdoche** (bộ phận thay toàn thể): "Her hands stopped shaking" thay cho "She became calm"
- **Container for contained**: "The kitchen went quiet" thay cho "They stopped talking"
- **Action for emotion**: "She folded the last shirt into the suitcase as if she were folding a letter she would never send" thay cho "She was sad while packing"

**Ví dụ**:
```
❌ Direct: "Sarah was devastated."
✅ Metonymy: "Sarah's coffee went cold on the counter. She didn't notice. She didn't notice anything for three days."

❌ Direct: "Daniel panicked."
✅ Metonymy: "Daniel's hands forgot how to hold the steering wheel."
```

### 3. NGŨ GIÁC VĂN CHƯƠNG (Sensory Writing) — 5 giác quan

**Rule**: Mỗi beat phải kích hoạt ≥2 giác quan (không chỉ thị giác).

**5 kênh**:
| Giác quan | Ví dụ Karma | Khi nào dùng |
|---|---|---|
| **Thị giác** | "The laptop glow turned her face blue" | Mọi scene (default) |
| **Thính giác** | "Somewhere down the hall, a vacuum hummed" | Tạo atmosphere + isolation |
| **Xúc giác** | "The ring caught on her sweater — she felt the prong bend" | Discovery moments |
| **Khứu giác** | "He came home smelling like cologne he'd never worn when he met her" | Betrayal clues |
| **Vị giác** | "Her coffee tasted like nothing. Everything tasted like nothing for a week." | Grief/numbness |

**Kỹ thuật nâng cao**:
- **Synesthesia** (trộn giác quan): "The silence tasted like metal"
- **Sensory contrast**: Scene ấm (mùi bánh mì) → chuyển lạnh (mùi bệnh viện) = emotional shift
- **Absent sensation**: "She couldn't smell the flowers on the nightstand. She couldn't smell anything." = numbness

### 4. NHỊP CÂU (Sentence Rhythm) — Ngắn/dài tạo nhịp thở

**Rule**: Rhythm phải match emotion. KHÔNG viết đều đều.

**Kỹ thuật**:
- **Staccato** (câu ngắn liên tiếp) = tension, shock, revelation:
  ```
  "He was married. He had been. The entire time."
  ```
- **Legato** (câu dài, mượt) = calm, domestic, nostalgic:
  ```
  "She was the kind of person who remembered every patient's birthday and packed her boyfriend's lunch every morning — turkey on rye, mustard, no pickles."
  ```
- **Caesura** (ngắt giữa câu) = dramatic pause:
  ```
  "The second unknown number? [pause 0.5s] Sarah clicked on it."
  ```
- **Anaphora** (lặp đầu câu) = building intensity:
  ```
  "She didn't cry. She didn't accuse him. She didn't scream. She did nothing."
  ```
- **Tricolon** (cụm 3) = satisfaction, completeness:
  ```
  "He lost his job, his license, and his apartment in the same month."
  ```

### 5. FORESHADOWING — Gieo mầm trước khi nổ

**Rule**: Beat 2 (Intro) phải chứa ≥2 foreshadow seeds cho Beat 4 (Betrayal).

**Kỹ thuật**:
- **Innocent detail becomes damning**: "He called her 'babe' in the way that still made her smile" → later: "He had been calling two women 'babe' for four years"
- **Negative foreshadow**: "She never questioned any of them. Why would she?" → reader feels dread
- **Object foreshadow**: Nhẫn xuất hiện beat 1 → nhẫn bị bỏ lại beat 6 → nhẫn thật xuất hiện epilogue
- **Callback**: Detail ở beat 2 return ở beat 7 with inverted meaning

**Ví dụ Karma pattern**:
```
Beat 2: "Daniel handled the investments — he was 'the numbers guy.'"
Beat 4: "The numbers guy had been miscounting on purpose for three years."
```

### 6. IRONY — Tạo khoảng cách giữa biết và không biết

**3 loại irony cho Karma**:

- **Dramatic irony** (viewer biết trước nhân vật):
  ```
  "Daniel told her he had a work trip coming up. Chicago, he said. A client meeting."
  [Viewer đã biết Chicago = Rachel]
  "Sarah nodded and smiled. She already knew about Chicago."
  ```

- **Situational irony** (kết quả ngược kỳ vọng):
  ```
  "The engagement ring wasn't fake because he was cheap. It was fake because he could never legally give her a real one."
  ```

- **Verbal irony** (nói ngược ý):
  ```
  "Daniel laughed. 'You're too good for me.' Sarah said, very quietly, 'Probably.'"
  ```

### 7. CHIASMUS & PARALLELISM — Đối xứng tạo sức nặng

**Rule**: Mỗi tập phải có ≥1 chiasmus (đảo cấu trúc) hoặc parallelism (lặp cấu trúc).

**Ví dụ**:
```
Parallelism:
"She didn't slam the door. She didn't cry. She locked the apartment behind her, dropped the key in the mailbox, and drove to the airport."

Chiasmus:
"Karma had a long memory. But Sarah had a longer one for what she deserved."
"She was not the person who forgot where she put her keys. She had never been that person. That person was the character Daniel had written for her."
```

### 8. MOTIF XUYÊN SUỐT — Vật/hành động lặp lại có biến đổi

**Rule**: Mỗi tập chọn 1 motif chính, xuất hiện ≥4 lần với nghĩa thay đổi.

**Pattern**: Object appears → object means X → object means Y → object means Z

**Ví dụ Ep01**:
- **Ring motif**: Ring đẹp (beat 2) → Ring fake (beat 3) → Ring bị bỏ (beat 6) → Ring thật từ James (epilogue)
- **Lunch motif**: Packing lunch = love (beat 2) → Lunch = habit of care for wrong person (beat 5) → James makes his own lunch (epilogue) = equal partnership

**Ví dụ mới cho Ep07 (Fitness Tracker)**:
- **Heart rate motif**: 72 BPM resting = Sarah's normal (beat 2) → 160 BPM 2AM = Daniel's lie (beat 3) → Sarah's heart rate stays steady when she finds out = she's already decided (beat 6) → James doesn't wear a tracker, he just asks "how are you feeling?" (epilogue)

### 9. KHOẢNG LẶNG (Negative Space) — Cái KHÔNG nói mạnh hơn cái nói

**Rule**: Mỗi beat climax phải có 1 moment im lặng — nhân vật KHÔNG làm gì.

**Kỹ thuật**:
- **Withheld reaction**: "Sarah closed the laptop. She heard the shower turn off. She didn't move." → cái không-di-chuyển = shock
- **Withheld confrontation**: "That night, she didn't ask him about it." → sức mạnh của kiềm chế
- **Withheld forgiveness**: "She didn't open it. She didn't delete it either." → email = unresolved but irrelevant
- **Withheld explanation**: "He didn't understand why she laughed. One day, when the time is right, she'll tell him." → intimacy of untold stories

**Negative space tạo**:
- Viewer tự fill in emotion (parasocial investment)
- Protagonist = powerful (không cần react = đã quyết)
- Suspense (không biết cô ấy sẽ làm gì)

### 10. ENDING ECHO — Câu cuối callback câu đầu (bookend)

**Rule**: CTA hoặc câu cuối epilogue phải echo hook opening — nhưng với nghĩa đảo ngược.

**Pattern**: Hook = pain. Echo = peace. Same words, different meaning.

**Ví dụ**:
```
Hook: "Sarah paid the phone bill every month for eight years. She never looked at it."
Echo: "Sarah, who paid a phone bill for eight years without looking, now looks at everything. Not because she doesn't trust James. But because she finally trusts herself."

Hook: "Daniel told everyone his wife was crazy."
Echo: "He has, on several occasions, told his friends that Sarah is the calmest, sharpest person he's ever met. She is. She always was."
```

## 📋 Script Quality Audit Checklist (chạy sau khi viết xong)

| # | Check | Target |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | ẩn dụ recurring (motif xuyên suốt) | ≥3 instances |
| 2 | Hoán dụ (emotion qua object/action) | ≥5 per script |
| 3 | Sensory details (≥2 giác quan per beat) | 20+ total |
| 4 | Sentence rhythm varies (staccato + legato) | Every beat |
| 5 | Foreshadowing seeds in Beat 2 | ≥2 seeds |
| 6 | Irony (dramatic + situational + verbal) | ≥3 instances |
| 7 | Chiasmus hoặc parallelism | ≥1 |
| 8 | Motif object transforms meaning 4+ times | 1 main motif |
| 9 | Negative space (withheld reaction/confrontation) | ≥2 moments |
| 10 | Ending echo callbacks hook | 1 clear echo |
| 11 | "First mistake" variant unique | Verified vs all eps |
| 12 | Forensic details (specific numbers/brands/places) | ≥5 |
| 13 | No cliché phrases ("heart sank", "world came crashing") | 0 clichés |
| 14 | Active voice > passive voice ratio | >80% active |

**Score**: Count checks passed. 12+ = publishable. 10-11 = needs polish. <10 = rewrite beats.

## 🔄 Revision workflow

Khi user yêu cầu "nâng cấp script":

1. Read full script
2. Run audit checklist → identify weak beats
3. For each weak beat, apply 2-3 techniques from above
4. Rewrite ONLY weak sections (không touch beats đã hay)
5. Verify motif consistency (thêm technique không phá motif cũ)
6. Re-run checklist → verify improvement
7. Update voice chunks (05-voice.md) nếu script text thay đổi

## ❌ Anti-patterns (TRÁNH)

| Cliché | Thay bằng |
|---|---|
| "Her heart sank" | "She felt her stomach drop" hoặc "The room tilted slightly" |
| "Time stood still" | Mô tả chi tiết cụ thể xung quanh (đồng hồ, tiếng gió, ánh sáng) |
| "She couldn't believe her eyes" | "She read the name three times. The letters didn't change." |
| "A wave of emotions" | Chọn 1 emotion cụ thể + 1 physical reaction |
| "Everything changed" | Show cái gì thay đổi cụ thể |
| "Little did she know" | Foreshadow subtler — let reader feel dread |
| "And that's when it all fell apart" | Cut — action speaks louder than narrator commentary |

## 📚 Reference — Kỹ thuật văn chương nâng cao

### Từ thơ ca ứng dụng vào prose
- **Enjambment** (câu tràn): Break câu ở chỗ bất ngờ → tạo suspense
- **Assonance** (lặp nguyên âm): "She closed the door. She closed her eyes. She closed that chapter." → âm "o" lặp = finality
- **Consonance** (lặp phụ âm): "The click of the lock. The tick of the clock." → âm "ck" = mechanical, final

### Từ điện ảnh ứng dụng vào narration
- **Smash cut** (chuyển cảnh đột ngột): Câu calm → câu shock không transition
- **Match cut** (chuyển cảnh qua vật giống): "She stared at the ring on the table" → "A year later, she stared at a different ring"
- **Montage** (chuỗi hành động nhanh): Tricolon liên tiếp = time passing fast
- **Long take** (cảnh dài không cắt): Đoạn dài không [pause] = immersion

### Từ âm nhạc ứng dụng vào pacing
- **Crescendo**: Câu ngắn → dài dần → climax
- **Diminuendo**: Sau climax → câu rút ngắn dần → silence
- **Rest** (nghỉ): [pause 2s] = musical rest, tạo anticipation
- **Ostinato** (lặp nền): Repeat phrase như "She didn't cry. She didn't accuse him." = emotional bassline
