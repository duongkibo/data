## 🎯 Mission

Build the most cathartic karma-story channel on YouTube. Audience cảm thấy:
1. **Validated** (their pain is normal)

## 🎯 Differentiator: "Cold Karma"

### Other channels (active revenge)
- "How I destroyed my ex"
- "My pettiest revenge ever"
- → Active = potentially un-sympathetic protagonist

### Karma Caught Up (passive karma)
- "She walked away. Then karma caught up."
- "I did nothing. The universe handled it."
- → Passive = sympathetic protagonist + brand-safe drama

**Key script element**: At 50% mark, protagonist makes **conscious choice NOT to act**. Universe does the work in second half. This is the USP.

## 🛡️ Compliance & Safety

### Legal protections
- ❌ NEVER use real names
- ❌ NEVER use real photos
- ❌ NEVER claim "based on true story" without disclaimer
- ✅ Disclaimer cuối video: *"This story is fictional, dramatized for entertainment. Any resemblance to real persons is coincidental."*
- ✅ Inspired by Reddit (no copy-paste)
- ✅ Names changed: use neutral names (Sarah, Mark, etc.)

### YouTube policy 2026
- ✅ No active revenge → brand-safe
- ✅ Original AI-generated content (creative input visible)
- ✅ No defamation
- ✅ No graphic violence
- ❌ No real people targeted

### Content safety
- Drama yes, violence no
- Emotional yes, graphic no
- Karma yes, vendetta no
