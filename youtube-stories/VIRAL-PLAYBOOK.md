## 🧠 Triết lý viral 2026

**3 variables** quyết định viral cho long-form story:
1. **CTR thumbnail + title** (get clicks) — 4-8% = viral tier
2. **Avg View Duration ≥70%** (keep viewers) — algo signal #1
3. **Shareability** (social lift) — comments + shares drive secondary reach

→ Tối ưu cả 3 → algo push mạnh → viral.

## 🎨 2. THUMBNAIL VIRAL FORMULAS

### Formula 1: Emotion close-up + 2-word text
```
[Female face extreme close-up devastated]
+ [2-word shock text TOP-LEFT]
+ [Single symbolic object bottom-right]
```
**CTR target**: 7-10%
**Example**: Sarah devastated + "FAKE RING" + ring icon

### Formula 2: Before/After split
```
[Happy scene LEFT] | [Devastated scene RIGHT]
+ [Contrast label top]
```
**CTR target**: 6-9%
**Example**: Wedding day LEFT | Empty bedroom RIGHT + "1 HOUR LATER"

### Formula 3: Mysterious object focus
```
[Macro object close-up] (ring, letter, phone screen)
+ [Question text]
+ [Devastated face background blur]
```
**CTR target**: 5-8%
**Example**: Ring close + "FAKE?" + Sarah crying blurred

### Formula 4: Shocking discovery moment
```
[Character mid-reaction] (hand to mouth, eyes wide)
+ [Action text top]
```
**CTR target**: 7-11%
**Example**: Sarah hand over mouth + "SHE FOUND OUT"

### Color rules
- **80% dark background** (navy, charcoal)
- **20% warm accent** (gold, burgundy)
- **Yellow text** only (#FFED00) — highest contrast
- **Black stroke 6-8px** around text
- **Drop shadow** heavy

### Text rules
- **2-3 words MAX**
- ALL CAPS
- Font: Anton / Bebas Neue / Impact
- Size: 120-150pt (scale 320×180 mini test)

### A/B test protocol
- Gen **3 variants** per episode
- Upload all 3 → YouTube A/B test (sau 1k subs)
- 7 days → declare winner CTR cao nhất
- Save winner pattern vào `analytics/thumbnail-winners.md`

## 📝 3. TITLE SEO FORMULAS

### Formula 1: Confession + Consequence (HIGHEST CTR)
```
"She [did X]. Then [unexpected consequence]."
```
- **Example**: "She walked away. Then the universe made him pay."
- **Length**: 50-65 chars

### Formula 2: Number + Betrayal
```
"[N] [time units] of [relationship]. Then [discovery]."
```
- **Example**: "7 years of marriage. One email changed everything."
- **Length**: 55-70 chars

### Formula 3: Discovery + Gap
```
"She found [small thing]. The truth was [bigger hint]."
```
- **Example**: "She found a fake ring. The truth was much worse."
- **Length**: 50-65 chars

### Formula 4: Question hook
```
"What would you do if [shocking scenario]?"
```
- **Example**: "What would you do if your engagement ring was a lie?"
- **Length**: 55-60 chars

### Formula 5: Time-skip reveal
```
"[N] years later, [consequence hit]"
```
- **Example**: "10 years later, karma finally caught up to him."
- **Length**: 50-60 chars

### Rules
- **Title length**: 50-65 chars (mobile display optimal)
- **Emoji**: 1-2 max (🦋 karma signature, 👁️ mystery, 💍 wedding)
- **Include "Karma Caught Up"** or "| Karma Story" branding cho recognition
- **Curiosity gap** — KHÔNG reveal twist trong title
- **Avoid**: clickbait extreme (penalty), ALL CAPS (spam), numbers at start

## 📊 4. RETENTION ENGINEERING (60%+ midpoint target)

### Critical retention points

| Time | Target retention | Action |
|---|---|---|
| **0:00-0:15** (hook) | 90%+ | Strong hook + visual shock |
| **0:45** (end intro) | 80%+ | Character relatable |
| **2:30** (betrayal) | 75%+ | Pain peak |
| **5:30** (pivot) | 70%+ | KEY — walk away decision |
| **7:00** (karma starts) | 65%+ | Satisfaction begins |
| **9:30** (downfall) | 60%+ | Climax |
| **11:00** (epilogue) | 55%+ | Warmth |
| **11:30** (CTA) | 50%+ | Subscribe hook |

### Retention tactics per beat

#### Beat 1 Hook (0:00-0:15)
- **Visual shock** first frame (ring on table, dramatic lighting)
- **Text overlay** at 0:02 ("FAKE RING")
- **Voice enters** at 0:05 (let visual land)
- **Music soft** 0:00, enter at 0:10

#### Beat 4 Betrayal (2:30-4:00) — PAIN PEAK
- **Slow zoom** on devastating moment (email reveal)
- **Music swell** starts 2:25
- **Pause 3 giây** at reveal ("He was already married.")
- **Cut to black** 0.3s post-reveal for emotional weight
- **Subtitle emphasis** on key betrayal words

#### Beat 6 Pivot (5:30-7:00) — KEY
- **Music shift** cool → warm (lift hopeful)
- **Color grade** cool blue → warm transition (keyframe)
- **Voice tone** shift từ heavy → hopeful
- **Visual**: Sunrise sequence (Ken Burns slow zoom)
- **THIS IS WHERE viewers decide to stay** → must feel relief

#### Beat 7 Karma (7:00-9:30) — SATISFACTION BUILD
- **Cuts faster** (every 3-5s) vs earlier slow cuts
- **Music builds** with each karma event
- **Specific details** ("lost house", "lost job", "$80k to IRS")
- **NO narration slow** — keep momentum

#### Beat 8 Downfall (9:30-10:30) — CLIMAX
- **Music peak** at 9:30
- **Final image** defeated antagonist (motel room)
- **Voice calm finality** ("Sarah hadn't lifted a finger.")

### Retention killers (AVOID)
- ❌ Slow monologue >30s without visual change
- ❌ Static shot >10s
- ❌ Repeated information
- ❌ Tangent/subplots
- ❌ Slow build past 2:30 mark

## 💬 5. ENGAGEMENT TACTICS (comments + shares)

### Comment prompts (embed in script)

> "If you've ever found out the person you trusted had a whole other life, leave a 🦋 below. Every butterfly is someone who chose peace over chaos."

→ Tạo comment wave với single emoji → algo likes
→ Threads form ("🦋 Me too, 3 years ago...")

### Comment reply protocol (first hour)

| Comment type | Reply template | Action |
|---|---|---|
| 🦋 only | "Welcome to the family. 💌" | Heart + reply |
| Shared story | "Thank you for trusting us. You chose peace. 🦋" | Heart + pin best |
| "Is this real?" | "Story dramatized, patterns very real. 🦋" | Heart |
| Angry/wanting revenge | "The universe has a better imagination than us. ⏳" | Heart |
| "More please!" | "Tuesday + Friday 9PM EST. 🔔" | Heart |

### Share triggers
Stories có **shareability cao** khi:
- ✅ Quotable line (epilogue wisdom)
- ✅ Relatable to wide audience
- ✅ Satisfying ending (not tragic)
- ✅ Short enough to recommend (10-12 min perfect)
- ✅ Clean content (send to friends OK)

### Pinned comment formula

[Question to spark discussion]

[Compliance reminder]

## 🎯 8. MILESTONE-BASED OPTIMIZATION

### 0-1k subs (Launch phase)
- Focus: **Consistency** 2/tuần
- Use: Formula 1 thumbnails, Formula 2-3 titles
- Engage: Reply mọi comment
- Test: Chưa A/B thumbnails

### 1k-10k subs (Growth phase)
- Focus: **CTR optimization**
- Use: A/B test 3 thumbnail variants
- Test: Title patterns
- Track: Which hook categories perform best

### 10k-50k subs (Scale phase)
- Focus: **Binge retention** (playlist strategy)
- Create: "Best Of" playlists by sub-niche
- Test: Longer videos (15-18 min variants)
- Collab: Reach other karma story channels

### 50k+ subs (Empire phase)
- Focus: **Diversification**
- Launch: Second channel (complementary niche)
- Sponsorships: Outreach to therapy apps, book clubs
- Merch: Mug, journal, "chose peace" apparel
