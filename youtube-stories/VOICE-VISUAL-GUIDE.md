## 🎨 VISUAL PRODUCTION

### Visual style guide

**Aesthetic**: Cinematic noir lite — not horror, not bright

**Color palette**:
- Primary: Cool blues #1E3A8A
- Secondary: Deep grays #1F2937
- Accent (single): Warm gold #FBBF24 OR blood red #DC2626
- Avoid: Bright saturated colors

**Lighting**:
- Low-key, single source
- Window light from side (Vermeer-style)
- Lamp light at night
- Avoid: 3-point studio, ring light

**Composition**:
- Wide isolation shots (protagonist alone in space)
- Tight emotional close-ups (eyes, hands, objects)
- Symbolic objects (phone face down, empty chair, ring)
- Avoid: Crowded scenes

## 🎬 Visual breakdown per beat

### Beat 1: Hook (15s)
**Shots**: 1-2 dramatic still frames
**Model**: Flux 2 Pro
**Style**: Single iconic image with text overlay

### Beat 2: Protagonist Intro (45s)
**Shots**: 3-4 lifestyle frames
**Model**: Nano Banana 2 (consistency)
**Style**: Warm domestic — coffee cup, morning light, smile, hands packing lunch

### Beat 3: The Crack (90s)
**Shots**: 4-5 ominous shots
**Model**: Kling 2.5 Turbo (slow zoom)
**Style**: Phone face down, empty side of bed, balcony silhouette, late night

### Beat 4: The Betrayal (90s)
**Shots**: 3 shots — discovery, realization, devastation
**Model**: Veo 3 close-up face (lip sync) + Kling 2.5 hands
**Style**: Phone glow on face, trembling hands, empty stare

### Beat 5: The Fall (90s)
**Shots**: 4-5 grief shots
**Model**: Minimax Hailuo (cheap atmospheric)
**Style**: Dark room, mirror reflection, untouched food, rain on window

### Beat 6: The Pivot (90s) ⭐
**Shots**: 5-6 shots — sunrise, decision, packing, plane
**Model**: Kling 2.5 + Flux for sunrise
**Style**: Light entering, calm purpose, sunrise key moment

### Beat 7: Karma Strikes (150s)
**Shots**: 8-10 shots — wedding, divorce, job loss, mother's house
**Model**: Mix all (Hailuo cheap for B-roll + Flux for hero)
**Style**: Things falling apart for antagonist

### Beat 8: Downfall (60s)
**Shots**: 3-4 final shots
**Model**: Flux + Kling
**Style**: Empty email, hardware store, basement, mother judging

### Epilogue (60s)
**Shots**: 4-5 happy shots
**Model**: Flux warm
**Style**: Sunny morning, new home, smile, real peace

### CTA (30s)
**Shots**: 1-2 brand frames
**Model**: Ideogram 3
**Style**: Butterfly graphic, channel logo, subscribe overlay

## 🎵 MUSIC PRODUCTION

### Tool: **CapCut Music Library** (FREE, không cần Suno)

### Why CapCut not Suno?
- ✅ Built-in CapCut Pro (no extra subscription)
- ✅ Copyright-safe trên YouTube
- ✅ Đủ chất lượng cinematic
- ✅ Search by mood/genre quick

### Workflow: Layer 3-4 short tracks
CapCut tracks thường 2-5 phút → cần layer multiple tracks cho 12-min episode.

| Beat | Search term | Length needed |
|---|---|---|

## 🎬 EDIT PROCESS (CapCut)

### Project setup
- Resolution: 1920×1080 (4K nếu có)
- Frame rate: 24fps (cinematic feel)
- Project: `karma-caught-up-ep01`

### Track layout
```
T1 Video    [hero shots Flux/Kling/Veo3]
T2 B-roll   [Hailuo atmospheric overlay 30%]
T3 Voice    [Charlotte narration full track]
T4 Music    [Suno bed 18%]
T5 SFX      [moments]
T6 Subs     [English burned-in optional]
T7 Effects  [letterbox, color grade, vignette]
```

### Cut pacing
- **Slow beats** (1-6): cuts every 5-8s
- **Karma beats** (7): cuts every 3-5s (build energy)
- **Downfall** (8): cuts every 2-4s (climax)
- **Epilogue**: cuts every 5-7s (settle)

### Color grade
- Cool blue tint overall
- Warm pop only on Epilogue
- Vignette 15% throughout
- Slight letterbox bars (cinematic)
- Film grain 5%

### SFX layers
- Phone notification (Beat 4)
- Heart beat slow (Beat 4-5)
- Door close (Beat 6 walk away)
- Sunrise birds (Beat 6 pivot)
- Wedding bells distant (Beat 7)
- Final email notification (Beat 8)

### Export
- 1080p 24fps H.264
- High bitrate
- AAC 256kbps audio
- ⚠️ NO watermark
