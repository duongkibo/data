- LEFT: Claire (cube voxel figure, cream cardigan, warm brown hair) — expression: controlled, decided, NOT crying. Facing slightly toward right.
- CENTER: Brown paper bag — large hero prop, slightly tilted, FOLDED TWICE visible at top
- RIGHT: Dark trash can with paper bag inside — cold color zone

**Text overlay:**
- TOP: "7 YEARS." — large bold white type with warm gold `#D4A547` drop shadow
- BOTTOM LEFT (under Claire): "EVERY SINGLE ONE." — bold white, smaller
- BOTTOM RIGHT (under trash): "THEN SHE FOUND OUT." — red `#DC2626` or cold blue text

**Color split:**
- Left half: Warm cream `#F3E9D2` + gold `#D4A547` (Claire's world)

- Center: Warm lighting on the bag — the pivot point
