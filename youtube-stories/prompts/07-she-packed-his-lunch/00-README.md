## Story Summary (8-beat)

1. **HOOK** — "Seven years. 2,555 lunches. She had no idea where they were going."
2. **INTRO** — Claire the librarian packs Greg's lunch daily. Happy marriage facade. Sourdough / turkey / green apple.
3. **CRACK** — Greg stays late. Phone face-down. She finds Chez Lune receipt. $43 for two. A Tuesday.
4. **BETRAYAL** — Checks his phone. Finds Natalie (paralegal, 14 months). Greg's text: "I usually toss it in the break room for whoever wants it." ⭐
5. **FALL** — Keeps making lunches. Plans quietly. Calls sister. "I know. But I need to do this right."
6. **PIVOT** — Aunt Dorothy dies → $42k + note "Do something that makes you impossible to ignore." Culinary course, food truck, divorce July. ⭐
7. **KARMA** — Truck launches September. Viral (12k likes). Natalie dumps Greg. Firm downsizes him. Investors approach Claire.
8. **DOWNFALL + EPILOGUE** — Restaurant Dorothy's opens March. Greg shows up April. Claire serves him The Usual ($12). Walks away. Claire thriving — 7 staff.
