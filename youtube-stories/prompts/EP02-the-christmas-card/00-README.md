# EP02 — "The Christmas Card"
# Production Folder — Karma Caught Up

---

## 📋 Story summary

Nina, 34, graphic designer in Columbus Ohio. Discovers — via a Christmas card mailed to her own house — that her husband Daniel has a second family: Amber, 33, dental hygienist, and their 6-year-old son Lucas. The affair began 6 years ago. Daniel's fake "Atlanta summit" trips funded a parallel life. Nina calls Amber (not to fight — to tell the truth). Two deceived women get lawyers. Court orders $92k backpay child support. Daniel loses savings, house, and job (fraudulent travel expenses exposed). Nina rebuilds: design award, new partner, and a two-person Christmas card — just her and Sophie.

**Emotional arc**: Christmas warmth → Devastating discovery → Cold quiet grief → Quiet pivot → Universe handles it → Peace earned
