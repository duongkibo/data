## Quality gate mỗi character

- [ ] Cube-based body visible (NOT raw Minecraft 1:2 ratio)
- [ ] Background pure white `#FFFFFF`
- [ ] Full body head to feet visible
- [ ] Signature color clear and distinct
- [ ] Face proportional — 2 dot eyes + simple mouth line
- [ ] All 3 characters DISTINCT from each other (different hair color + outfit color)
- [ ] Nina (cream) vs Daniel (charcoal) vs Amber (navy) — contrast clear
- [ ] Nina và Amber KHÔNG bị nhầm nhau (different hair color + palette)

**Fail** → Gen Variations 2-3 lần pick best.
