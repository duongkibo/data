## Story Summary (8-beat)

1. **HOOK** — "She trusted her babysitter with her children. She didn't think she needed to distrust her husband."
2. **INTRO** — Dara the capable stay-at-home mom, Marcus the reliable community dad, Emma the trusted 23-year-old babysitter of 14 months.
3. **CRACK** — Emma arrives dressed up. Kids mention she stayed for dinner. Marcus always late on Thursdays. Dara keeps notes for six weeks.
4. **BETRAYAL** — Dara skips conference, comes home early. Both cars in driveway. Eleven minutes in her own car. She goes in. Kitchen table. Three words: "Please leave now." Sits until morning. ⭐
5. **FALL** — The long night at the table. Clarity like after a glass breaks. Eighteen entries on her phone. The children's routines. She gives herself one day.
6. **PIVOT** — Calls sister → attorney Patricia → eighteen entries as documentation. Enrolls in the shelved certification course. Moves through the weeks doing what must be done. ⭐
7. **KARMA** — Emma pregnant. Her family cuts contact. Marcus had no plan for Emma. Divorce 14 months. Dara: primary custody + house (inheritance down payment). Marcus: split three ways financially. Soccer coaching ends quietly.
8. **DOWNFALL** — Marcus files custody of new baby — second proceeding, second judge, same character record. Financially split three ways. Community reputation quietly revised.
9. **EPILOGUE** — Certification finished. Job in June. New babysitter through agency. Every evening home to the kids, lights on in every room. The room is hers.
