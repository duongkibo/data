# Production Folder — Karma Caught Up

## Story summary

Diane, 36, accountant in Richmond VA. For 8 years she paid the joint phone bill without looking. In February, a bank duplicate-charge flag makes her open the actual itemized statement. She builds a spreadsheet: 247 calls, one Nashville number, 615 area code, always after 9pm, six months running. She dials it. Serena answers. Both women are blindsided — Marcus told Serena he was separated. Diane prints all 43 pages, leaves them on the kitchen counter, and goes to work on her audit: 4am, joint accounts, hotel receipts, jewelry charges. By 8am she is at First National. Three days later she is legally separated, the house is on market, and Marcus's lawyer is mediocre. Karma arrives via Patrick — Serena's husband works commercial roofing, Marcus loses 3 bids through a single phone call. Two years later: Diane owns her firm, has a balcony garden, and checks the phone bill every month. Not afraid. Just accurate.

**Emotional arc**: Domestic trust → Quiet dread → Devastating discovery → Cold precision → Karma collects → Peaceful ownership
