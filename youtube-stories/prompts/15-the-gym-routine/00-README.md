## Story Summary (8-beat)

1. **HOOK** — "Six months. Five a.m. He came home looking exactly the same. The gym had never heard his name."
2. **INTRO** — Mara the project manager, Daniel the husband. He announces gym life. She's proud.
3. **CRACK** — Gym clothes never smell. He can't name exercises. Water bottle always full. She notices. ⭐ Hero prop: blue Hydro Flask.
4. **BETRAYAL** — She goes to Velocity Fitness. His name is not in the check-in system. Has never been. ⭐
5. **FALL** — She doesn't react. She plans. Calls attorney Stephanie Okafor. The house is hers. Discovers VM Personal Training LLC on joint credit card.
6. **PIVOT** — Files divorce June. Confronts Daniel calmly. Sends Veronica one professional email. Everything set in motion. ⭐
7. **KARMA** — Veronica's husband Owen finds out via legal process. Owen files. Sublease on Meridian expires — Owen doesn't renew. VM Training collapses. Daniel loses house, dog, equity, Veronica.
8. **DOWNFALL + EPILOGUE** — Mara repaints bedroom sage green. One year later joins real gym. 5am. Comes home sweating. Green water bottle — empty. Breakfast made.
