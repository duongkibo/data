## 📋 Story summary

Maya, 29, kindergarten teacher in Austin. Discovers — 2 days before her own baby shower — that her boyfriend Ryan has been building a nursery for another woman's baby simultaneously. He lied to both women. Both leave him the same day. Karma collects: HR complaint, job loss, family cutoff. Maya moves forward with Lily and Caleb.

**Emotional arc**: Domestic happiness → Devastating discovery → Quiet departure → Universe handles it → Peaceful new life
