## CapCut Edit Workflow

### Track layout

```
Track 1 Audio  : chunk1.mp3 → chunk2.mp3 → chunk3.mp3 (merge, no gap)
Track 2 Audio  : BGM CapCut library (-18dB under voice)
Track 1 Video  : C01 → C34 aligned với voice beats
Track 2 Video  : S01-S15 Ken Burns stills in gaps
Track 3 Video  : Subtitles auto-generated
```

### Beat-accurate timestamp reference

| Beat | Scenes | Timeline | Color grade |
|---|---|---|---|
| Beat 1 — Hook | C01 | 0:00–0:15 | WARM cream gold |
| Beat 2 — Intro | C02–C03 | 0:15–1:00 | WARM cream gold |
| Beat 3 — Crack | C04–C08 | 1:00–2:30 | WARM fading → cool slate |
| Beat 4 — Betrayal | C09–C13 | 2:30–4:00 | DARK COLD #0B1426 |

### Step-by-step

1. **Import voice** — 3 chunks lên Track 1 Audio, merge sequential, no crossfade between chunks
2. **Import 34 video clips** (C01→C34) lên Track 1 Video, align theo beat timestamps above:
   - C01: 0:00–0:15 (Hook — party planning reveal, knowing tone)
   - C02–C03: 0:15–1:00 (Intro — Vivian getting ready, Daniel at prior event)
   - C04–C08: 1:00–2:30 (Crack — late nights, hotel receipt, party setup)
   - C09–C13: 2:30–4:00 (Betrayal — 8:47 PM, door, Vivian's walk, water)
   - C14–C17: 4:00–5:30 (Fall — goodbyes, empty room, Daniel waiting, dress off)
   - C18–C21: 5:30–7:00 (Pivot — packing bag, car, kitchen floor, Monday)
   - C22–C28: 7:00–9:30 (Karma — divorce, house, studio, Rosalie, contacts, Claire)
   - C29: 9:30–10:30 (Downfall — Daniel's diminishment, apartment, groceries)
   - C30–C33: 10:30–11:30 (Epilogue — new apartment, 8 guests, Petra's toast, window)
   - C34: 11:30–12:00 (CTA — butterfly card)
3. **Insert 15 Ken Burns stills** vào gaps giữa clips:
   - Scale keyframe: 100% → 115% over duration
   - S01 (20s), S02 (15s), S03 (25s), S04 (20s), S05 (25s) — Beats 3-4
   - S06 (15s), S07 (20s), S08 (20s) — Beats 5-6
   - S09 (20s), S10 (20s), S11 (20s), S12 (20s) — Beat 7
   - S13 (15s), S14 (20s), S15 (40s) — Epilogue
4. **BGM** CapCut library:
   - Genre: Soft cinematic tension / Intimate piano / Corporate minimal
   - Beat 1-5: Cooler, more minimal (understated tension — party feels wrong from the start)
   - Beat 6-8 + Epilogue: Warmer, lifting — piano moving to major key
   - Volume: **-18dB** (voice stays dominant at -6dB)
   - Fade in 3s at start, fade out 5s at end
5. **Color grade** (beat-accurate, mandatory):
   - Beat 1-2: Temp +2, Saturation +3 (warm cream gold — Vivian's confident world)
   - Beat 3: Temp -1, Saturation -2 (cool slate entering — something building wrong)
   - Beat 4: Temp -4, Saturation -6 (dark cold — the door, the silence, the water)
   - Beat 5: Temp -4, Saturation -7 (dark cold — fall, transitioning at pivot)
   - Beat 6: Temp 0, Saturation 0 (turning warm neutral — packing bag, kitchen floor)
   - Beat 7: Temp +1, Saturation +2 (neutral warming — house, settlement, Rosalie)
   - Beat 8: Temp -2, Saturation -3 (cool gray — Daniel's diminishment)
   - Epilogue: Temp +4, Saturation +5 (full warm sage — her birthday, her terms)
6. **Subtitles**: Auto-generate English → manual fix these names:
   - Vivian (not Vivienne / Vivien)
   - Daniel (not Danielle / Danyel)
   - Petra (not Peta / Peetra)
   - Rosalie (not Rosalie / Rosalee)
   - Hawthorn (not Hawthorne / Hauthorn)
   - Claire (not Clair / Clare)
   - La Table du Soir (not La Table du Swah / La Tableau)
   - Arden Grand (not Ardon / Arden Grande)
   - Westport (not West Port)
7. **DUTCH check**: Verify DUTCH appears ONLY in C09B, C09C, C10B, C10C, C11B — verify NONE in C12 onward
8. **Export**:
   - Resolution: 1920×1080
   - Frame rate: 30fps
   - Codec: H.264
   - Bitrate: 10 Mbps
   - Format: MP4

**Save**: `media/final/EP18-the-birthday-party-final.mp4`

## YouTube Studio Upload Steps

### 1. Upload file

YouTube Studio → Create → Upload videos → chọn `EP18-the-birthday-party-final.mp4`

### 2. Details tab

- **Title**: paste từ `07-title-description.md` (V1 primary)
- **Description**: paste full từ `07-title-description.md`
- **Thumbnail**: upload `v1.png` (primary) — lưu v2, v3 cho A/B test sau 1k subs
- **Playlists**: "Karma Caught Up Stories" → add
- **Audience**: No, it's not made for kids
- **Age restriction**: No
- More options:
  - Tags: paste 15 tags
  - Language: English
  - Category: **Entertainment** (hoặc People & Blogs)
  - License: Standard YouTube License
  - ✅ Allow embedding
  - ✅ Publish to subscriptions feed + notify
  - Shorts sampling: OFF

### 3. Video elements tab

- **Subtitles**: Upload SRT từ CapCut export (English)
- **End screen** (add at 11:10):
  - Subscribe button (bottom-right)
  - Next video (bottom-left, "best for viewer")
- **Cards**:
  - 1 card at **6:00** (Pivot moment — divorce filed Monday) → link subscribe

### 4. Checks tab

- Copyright scan: wait green ✅
- Ad suitability: target Green — nếu Yellow request manual review

### 5. Visibility tab

- **Schedule**: Tuesday 9:00 PM EST hoặc Friday 9:00 PM EST
- ✅ Set as Premiere (optional — tạo pre-launch hype)
- Click **Schedule**
