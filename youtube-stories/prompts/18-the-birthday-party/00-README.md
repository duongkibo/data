# EP18 — "The Birthday Party"
## @KarmaCaughtUp · Long-form · 11:30 · Voxel Art

---

## Story Summary (4 sentences)

Vivian, a 35-year-old event planner with her own firm, arrives at her own surprise birthday party to find her husband has also been running a parallel relationship — which announces itself at the door at 8:47 PM in the form of a visibly pregnant woman named Claire. Vivian greets her by name, invites her inside, offers her water, and stays composed for every remaining minute of the party while forty people watch. When the last guest leaves, Vivian packs her husband's bag, calls him a car, and files for divorce on Monday morning. She keeps the house, keeps her business, keeps her clarity — and her next birthday, the one she plans herself, is small and exactly right.
