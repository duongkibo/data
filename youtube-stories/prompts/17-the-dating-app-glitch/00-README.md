## Story Summary (8-beat)

1. **HOOK** — A Tinder notification appears on Nadia's phone through a shared Apple ID sync. Gone in seconds. She files it.
2. **INTRO** — Nadia the employment attorney. 5-year marriage to Marcus, tech company director. Shared Apple ID from the early days.
3. **CRACK** — She researches the notification. Opens iCloud backup via their shared Apple ID. Finds three apps: Tinder, Bumble, Hinge.
4. **BETRAYAL** — 14-month history. 22 matches. Three names from his company's org chart. Claire from marketing. Priya from partnerships. She's an employment attorney. She knows exactly what this means. ⭐
5. **FALL** — 3 weeks of precise documentation. Screenshots, timeline, organized folder. Sister says burn it down. Nadia says: I'm doing it correctly.
6. **PIVOT** — Attorney colleague confirms condo goes to Nadia (down payment from grandmother's inheritance). Nadia anonymizes evidence and drafts HR email — doesn't send yet. Papers served first. She tells Marcus what she knows. Then makes tea. ⭐
7. **KARMA** — Marcus calls 6x, texts 3x. Third text accuses Nadia of privacy violation — she forwards it to her attorney. Sends anonymized HR email. HR investigates. Marcus terminated. Claire resigns. Priya resigns. Divorce finalizes. Nadia keeps condo.
8. **DOWNFALL + EPILOGUE** — Marcus in a one-bedroom, "consulting." Nadia takes solo coastal trip. Turns off all notifications. Sits on the beach until dark. Doesn't miss a single thing.
