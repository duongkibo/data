[heavy] The next morning, Sarah called in sick to work. She dropped the girls at school. She came home and sat at the kitchen table with her laptop and a legal pad. [pause 0.5s] She did what she always did when the world stopped making sense. [pause 0.5s] She investigated. [pause 2s]

[pause 1s] There were charges at hotels in Denver, in Dallas, in Miami — cities on his regular routes. Different room-service orders for two. Flowers sent to addresses she didn't recognize. [pause 2s]
