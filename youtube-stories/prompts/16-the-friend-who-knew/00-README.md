## Story Summary (8-beat)

1. **HOOK** — "She stood at the altar crying. She gave the speech. She was also sleeping with the groom."
2. **INTRO** — Iris the graphic designer. 14-year friendship with Maya. Wedding 3 years ago. Maya was MOH.
3. **CRACK** — Iris notices Thursdays: Maya cancels every one. Daniel works late every one. One Thursday, she drives past a restaurant and sees them together in a booth.
4. **BETRAYAL** — Family tablet location history: 6 Thursdays, same hotel. Maya's Instagram geolocated from hotel. Next Thursday: Iris in parking lot with camera, timestamps them walking in together. ⭐
5. **FALL** — Tells sister. Plans quietly. Books attorney consult. Stays completely composed.
6. **PIVOT** — Attorney Helen confirms apartment is pre-marital asset. Papers served Tuesday. Daniel's one-word answer from Iris: "Thursdays." ⭐
7. **KARMA** — Divorce finalized. Apartment stays with Iris. Rowan finds out, ends engagement publicly. Maya texts, Iris blocks. Freelance career explodes — 5 clients, studio in second bedroom.
8. **DOWNFALL + EPILOGUE** — Daniel and Maya implode in 4 months. Iris moves to new city. Book group. Callum at a dinner party. "Thursdays are fine now."
