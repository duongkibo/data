## Story Summary (8-beat)

1. **HOOK** — A thank-you card in the mail. Addressed to Marcus. In handwriting Nina has known since she was seven. No return address. Patricia.
2. **INTRO** — Nina the English teacher, Marcus the charmer at every family table. Eight years married. Seven-year-old Lily. And Patricia — Diane's best friend of 22 years, front pew at the wedding, hospital waiting room when Lily was born.
3. **CRACK** — Nina calls her mother casually, asks if Patricia has been in touch with Marcus. Her mother goes quiet. Two seconds. That silence tells her everything. ⭐
4. **BETRAYAL** — Nina drives to her mother's house. Four hours at the kitchen table. Diane pieces it together too — the changed energy, the misread text, the too-specific comments. The betrayal is Diane's too. ⭐
5. **FALL** — Nina sits with the full weight of it. Four hours. Yellow legal pad. She drives home at 1:30 AM, kisses Lily, makes a list in her head. Does not sleep.
6. **PIVOT** — Calls Claire, the family attorney. Pre-marital house — Nina's name only, grandmother's estate. "Do not change your behavior. Let the record build itself." Three weeks. Document quietly.
7. **KARMA** — Divorce filed. House stays with Nina (pre-marital). Hidden credit card in discovery (restaurants, hotels, jewelry). Marcus served at his office. Diane calls Patricia: "I know. Don't call." Four words. Patricia's entire social network — gone.
8. **DOWNFALL + EPILOGUE** — Marcus loses his family context entirely. Patricia and Marcus end in 5 months. Patricia moves away quietly. Christmas: Nina, Lily, Diane, Rachel — four women. His seat replaced by a potted olive tree.
