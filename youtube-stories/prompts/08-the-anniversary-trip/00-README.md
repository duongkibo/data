## Story Summary (8-beat)

1. **HOOK** — "He booked the anniversary trip to save their marriage. The hotel saved her from it."
2. **INTRO** — Maya the marketing manager, Daniel the architect. 7 years together, 4 married. He surprises her with the Ashwood Inn — a place she mentioned once, two years ago. She thinks it means he was listening.
3. **CRACK** — Concierge: "Welcome back, Mr. Harlow." He knows the layout, the quiet corner table, the spa hours. Small things. Each explainable. All wrong.
4. **BETRAYAL** — Spa receptionist hands Maya the wrong booking confirmation — Daniel's visit, 12 days prior. "Couples deep tissue — second visit." Hotel manager shows reservation log: Guest: two. Duration: three nights. ⭐
5. **FALL** — Maya finishes the trip. Swims. Eats room service. Tips well. Says nothing. Thinks constantly.
6. **PIVOT** — Photographs the confirmation. Emails it to herself from hotel lobby wifi. Attorney call Thursday: "I want to document everything." ⭐
7. **KARMA** — Divorce filed. Discovery reveals hidden joint account in Serena's name. House is Maya's only. She keeps it. Daniel asked to resign from firm. Serena leaves.
8. **DOWNFALL + EPILOGUE** — Daniel calls Ashwood Inn to complain. Manager logs the call. Court references it: "not remorseful in character." Maya books new solo trip. New city. Marketing director role. Christmas cards to hotel manager. Every year.
