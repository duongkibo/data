## Quality gate mỗi character

- [ ] Cube-based body visible (NOT raw Minecraft 1:2 ratio)
- [ ] Background pure white `#FFFFFF`
- [ ] Full body head to feet visible
- [ ] Signature color clear and distinct per character
- [ ] Face proportional — 2 dot eyes + simple mouth line
- [ ] All 3 characters DISTINCT from each other (different hair + outfit palette)
- [ ] Marcus (navy/cream) vs Derek (dark grey) vs Jade (cream dress/dark hair) — contrast clear
- [ ] Marcus and Derek do NOT look identical — different build, different jacket color, different expression energy
- [ ] Jade wedding dress reads clearly as formal, not casual

**Fail** → Gen Variations 2-3 lần pick best.
