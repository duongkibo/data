# EP05 — "The Best Man Speech"
# Production Folder — Karma Caught Up

---

## Story summary

Marcus, 31, structural engineer, Asheville NC. Methodical, precise, trusts very few people. Engaged 2 years 11 months to Jade. Best man: Derek, a 12-year college friend with 3 years sobriety before a relapse.

At the reception — 74 guests, Riverside Gardens venue, October — Derek stands, picks up a glass, and gives a speech no one expected. Midway through, hand shaking, he confesses into a live microphone: "Jade and I have been seeing each other. For fourteen months."

Marcus goes very still. He does not explode. He leaves at 6:47 PM. He goes back to work Monday 7am, tie on, phone calls made, 61% of deposits recovered. The recreation center project — 8,400 sq ft — becomes his anchor. It clears first submission with no revisions.

Derek and Jade go public. Courthouse marriage. Derek relapses 8 months later. Jade moves out. Derek loses his job. His LinkedIn goes private. The recreation center opens in March. Marcus is named project lead. He is seeing someone new, careful, early days.

"The speech showed him which one was worth building."

**Emotional arc**: October warmth → Ceremony peak → Betrayal mid-speech → Cold still grief → Methodical pivot → Universe handles it → Earned quiet
