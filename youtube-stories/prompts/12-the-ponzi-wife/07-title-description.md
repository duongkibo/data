| Subs gained per 100 views | 2 | 6 |

**Note**: True crime content typically gets higher CTR and longer AVD than fiction. "TRUE STORY" badge should boost CTR by 15-25% vs fiction thumbnails.

## If underperform (24h check)
- CTR < 4% -> swap thumbnail V1 -> V2 or V3
- AVD < 5 min -> analyze drop-off point in Analytics (likely Beat 3-4 transition)
- Likes < 3% -> story tone may be too dark, lighten next episode
- Comments low -> add poll card at 2:00 mark asking "Would you have asked the question?"
