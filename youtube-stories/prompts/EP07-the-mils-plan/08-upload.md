## CapCut Edit Workflow

### Track layout

```
Track 1 Audio  : chunk1.mp3 → chunk2.mp3 → chunk3.mp3 (merge, no gap)
Track 2 Audio  : BGM CapCut library (-18dB under voice)
Track 1 Video  : C01 → C34 aligned with voice beats (3 clips per scene → trim to 3s each)
Track 2 Video  : S01-S15 Ken Burns stills in key emotional gaps
Track 3 Video  : Subtitles auto-generated → manual corrections
```

### Step-by-step

1. **Import voice** — 3 chunks onto Track 1 Audio, merge sequential, no crossfade between chunks
2. **Import 34 video clips** (C01→C34) onto Track 1 Video, each scene = 3 clips × 3s = 9s/scene:
   - C01: 0:00–0:15 (Hook — phone voicemail glow)
   - C02–C05: 0:15–1:00 (Intro — Claire's office, garden, married life, Renata meeting)
   - C06–C10: 1:00–2:30 (Crack — Owen home, Renata's calls, visits, rewrite, pattern recognition)
   - C11–C15: 2:30–4:00 (Betrayal — Sunday kitchen, incompatibility, children lie, separation, window-watcher)
   - C16–C19: 4:00–5:30 (Fall — Owen moves out, work/home duality, pattern analysis, voicemail archive found)
   - C20–C24: 5:30–7:00 (Pivot — VM3, VM5, written log, the call, handing Owen the phone)
   - C25–C31: 7:00–9:30 (Karma — Owen listening, "how long", backyard call, certainty, no-contact, network backfire, Christmas)
   - C32–C33: 9:30–10:30 (Downfall — Facebook post 1 like, Claire's quiet)
   - C34: 10:30–11:30 (Epilogue + CTA — nameplate, lunch call, voicemails still saved)
3. **Insert 15 Ken Burns stills** in key emotional gaps:
   - Scale keyframe in CapCut: 100% → 115% over duration (slow zoom)
   - S01 (20s) — window-watcher realization [Beat 4 peak] → after C15
   - S02 (15s) — community garden origin warmth [Beat 2] → after C03
   - S03 (15s) — Owen's wedding photo on desk [Beat 2] → after C04
   - S04 (10s) — Renata's first smile at the door [Beat 2–3] → after C05
   - S05 (15s) — Renata leaning toward Owen in living room [Beat 3] → during C08
   - S06 (20s) — Claire's written log spread [Beat 6] → after C22
   - S07 (15s) — Voicemail archive, 8 entries on screen [Beat 5] → after C19
   - S08 (20s) — VM3 text on screen [Beat 6] → after C20
   - S09 (25s) — VM5 text on screen [Beat 6 PEAK] → after C21
   - S10 (20s) — Owen in backyard, Claire through window [Beat 7] → during C27
   - S11 (20s) — No-contact order document, Owen's signature [Beat 7] → after C29
   - S12 (20s) — Christmas family gathering, Renata's empty chair [Beat 7] → after C31
   - S13 (15s) — Facebook post "1 like 0 comments" [Beat 8] → during C32
   - S14 (15s) — Clinical Supervisor nameplate [Epilogue] → after C34 begins
   - S15 (40s) — Phone voicemail archive, still all 8 saved [Epilogue FINAL] → end card
4. **BGM** CapCut library:
   - Genre: Ambient electronic / Soft cinematic tension
   - Beat 1-2: Warm minimal piano (Claire's good baseline)
   - Beat 3-5: Cold strings, tension building, subtle dissonance
   - Beat 6: Shift at voicemail reveal — strings turning, warmth entering
   - Beat 7: Building resolution — steady, not triumphant
   - Beat 8: Quiet — almost silence with soft tone
   - Epilogue: Warm minimal piano returning — full circle
   - Volume: **-18dB** (voice stays dominant at -6dB)
   - Fade in 3s at start, fade out 5s at end
5. **Color grade** (beat-accurate, mandatory):
   - Beat 1-2: Temp +3, Saturation +4 (warm cream gold — the good baseline)
   - Beat 3: Temp -1, Saturation -2 (warm cooling — pattern entering)
   - Beat 4: Temp -5, Saturation -7 (DARK COLD #0B1426 — betrayal maximum)
   - Beat 5: Temp -4, Saturation -6 (dark navy — quiet devastation)
   - Beat 6: Temp 0, Saturation 0 (turning warm — pivot begins)
   - Beat 7: Temp -1, Saturation -2 (cold gray institutional — karma in motion)
   - Beat 8: Temp -2, Saturation -3 (neutral cool — the Facebook post)
   - Epilogue: Temp +5, Saturation +5 (FULL WARM sage — restored life)
6. **Subtitles**: Auto-generate English → manually fix these names:
   - Claire (not Clara / Clair / Clayre)
   - Owen (not Owin / Owain)
   - Renata (not Renatta / Ranata / Renata's)
   - Diane (not Dianne / Dian)
   - Marcus (not Markus / Marcuse)
   - "fundamental incompatibility" (not "incompatible" — the exact phrase)
   - voicemail (not voice mail / voicemal)
   - "no-contact" (not "no contact" — hyphenated)
7. **DUTCH verification**:
   - Verify DUTCH only in: C12B · C13B · C13C · C14B · C15B
   - Verify NO DUTCH in: C16–C34, all Ken Burns stills, Epilogue
8. **Export**:
   - Resolution: 1920×1080
   - Frame rate: 30fps
   - Codec: H.264
   - Bitrate: 10 Mbps
   - Format: MP4
