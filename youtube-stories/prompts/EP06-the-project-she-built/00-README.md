# EP06 — "The Project She Built"
## @KarmaCaughtUp · Long-form · 11:30 · Voxel Art

---

## Story Summary (4 sentences)

Nadia spent six years at Veltara building things that made other people look brilliant — until Marcus, her Senior Director, walked into an all-hands with forty slides of her work and presented every frame as his own. When Nadia's name appeared fourth in an eleven-person footnote and Marcus walked out with a director's bonus, she said nothing — she just opened her laptop, exported every Figma file with its version history, and resigned on day twelve. Marcus then called HR and told them she had "communication concerns," but the timestamps in the version history told a different story — ninety-four percent of the artboards bore her name, and the design community found out. Crestline UX LLC now clears $340k a quarter; Marcus was quietly moved lateral to a smaller team with no client-facing work.
