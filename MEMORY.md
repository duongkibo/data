## 🚨 Production math reality

**Video 11:30 = 690s cần**:
- NOT 60 clips × 10s (expensive + overkill)
- ✅ **Hybrid**: ~15 animated clips (Kling 10s) + ~33 still frames với Ken Burns effect
- Total: 200s animated + 490s Ken Burns stills = 690s ✅

**Ken Burns** = CapCut keyframe scale 100% → 115% trên still trong 12-18s → giả lập motion.

**Cost**:
- 33 stills × ~$0.04 = $1.20
- 15 animated clips × $0.20 = $3.00
- Voice + music = $0.20
- **Total: ~$4.40/episode**
